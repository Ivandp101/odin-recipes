Garni Yarikh - Stuffed Eggplant

Description

Garni Yarikh is a speciality of the Azarbaijan province. Garni Yarikh is the Turkish name.

This is the kind of dish that seduces and beckons as silky eggplant collapses into a rich sauce.

Ingredients

  • 6 Italian or baby eggplant
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 10 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 6 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
  • Ground black pepper
  • 1/2 bunch parsley, tough stems trimmed, chopped (set a little aside for garnish)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced in half

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425F with the rack set in the center position.
  2. Line a large baking sheet with foil or parchment paper and lightly oil.
  3. Peel the eggplant in stripes, with the stem and cap on.
  4. Leaving 1 inch off the top and bottom, cut a shallow split lengthwise in the belly of the eggplant.
  5. Ensure that the cut does not pierce right through. and stop about 1 inch from the bottom.
  6. Place the eggplant in a large bowl.
  7. With a teaspoon drizzle a little of the olive oil in each slit and sprinkle generously with salt.
  8. Rub the rest of the olive oil all over the eggplant, and season with a good pinch of salt and a little black pepper.
  9. Place the eggplant on the baking sheet and bake for about 35 minutes, until fork tender.
  10. Set aside to cool.
