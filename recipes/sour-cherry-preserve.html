Sour Cherry Preserve

Description

This sour cherry preserve is delicious served with feta cheese on crostini as a wonderful Persian-inspired breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sour cherries, pitted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon rose water (optional)

Steps

  1. Place the sour cherries in a medium bowl.
  2. Add the sugar and mix.
  3. Partially cover and set aside for 3 hours or overnight to macerate.
  4. Place the macerated cherries in a heavy-bottomed medium pan, and add the lime juice.
  5. Bring to a quick boil over medium-high heat.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium low and simmer, stirring often - about 20-25 minutes until cherries soften.
  7. After this simmering, the mixture will have thickened, so now transfer the cherries to a glass jar.
  8. Keep reducing the juices for another five minutes, bearing in mind that the preserves will condsiderably thicken as they cool.
  9. Pour the juices over the cherries and add the rose water.
  10. Stir and set aside to cool to room temperature.
  11. Cover and refrigerate.
  12. Preserves should last up to a month in the fridge.
